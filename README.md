# 🚑 RouteCare

**RouteCare** is a healthcare transportation platform designed to help patients quickly and easily reach hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities when transportation is needed most.

## 🌍 Problem

In many rural and underserved areas, accessing reliable transportation during medical situations remains a significant challenge. Delays in reaching healthcare facilities can negatively impact patient outcomes and access to timely care.

## 💡 Solution

RouteCare connects patients with nearby drivers, providing a fast, reliable, and accessible way to travel to healthcare facilities.

The platform aims to:

* Reduce delays in accessing medical care.
* Connect patients with available drivers in real time.
* Improve healthcare accessibility in rural communities.
* Provide a safe, simple, and user-friendly experience.

## 🎯 Vision

To become the leading healthcare transportation platform, ensuring that every patient can access medical care when they need it most.

## 🚀 Features

* Medical transportation requests
* Smart driver matching
* Real-time notifications
* Emergency priority requests
* Location-based driver discovery
* Secure and reliable transportation
